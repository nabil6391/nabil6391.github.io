About

I started my career as an Android developer, responsible for the complete development lifecycle of the mobile applications including designing user interface, preparing content, programming and debugging. I understood how crucial documentation, version control, and effective team communication are in terms of having a sane workflow.

I prefer to spend much time in learning new features, presently which is feeling confident in both sides of the spectrum to develop Web Apps from back to front along with their mobile counterparts. ReactJS has become one of my favorite tools in development.

I would love to build best mobile experiences using Android, iOS and Progressive Web Apps (PWA) for your team.
